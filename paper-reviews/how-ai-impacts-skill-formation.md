# Paper Review

## Paper

How AI Impacts Skill Formation

## Why This Paper Matters

This paper explores one of the most important questions surrounding AI adoption:

Does AI improve learning and expertise development, or does it reduce opportunities for skill acquisition?

While AI systems may increase productivity, productivity alone is not sufficient if long-term expertise declines.

## Key Insight

The paper suggests that AI assistance can improve immediate performance while potentially reducing opportunities for deeper learning and independent problem-solving.

## Relevance To My Research

This finding has important implications for AI-assisted software development.

If developers increasingly rely on AI systems for coding, debugging, and architecture decisions, organisations may need to consider whether productivity gains come at the expense of long-term expertise development.

## Open Questions

* How does AI affect security judgement?
* How does AI affect incident response capabilities?
* How does AI affect infrastructure troubleshooting skills?
* How should organisations balance productivity and learning?

## Personal Reflection

The future of AI-assisted work should not be measured solely by output. It should also be measured by the quality of human expertise that remains after AI is introduced into the workflow.
