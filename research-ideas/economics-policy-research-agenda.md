# Economics & Policy Research Agenda

## Core Theme

Understanding how increasingly capable AI systems affect expertise, productivity, security, governance, and organisational resilience.

## Priority Questions

### AI and Skill Formation

Does AI improve expertise development or reduce opportunities for learning?

### Productivity versus Security

Do productivity gains outweigh long term security costs?

### Organisational Resilience

How does AI affect the ability of organisations to respond to incidents and uncertainty?

### Governance

What governance mechanisms should accompany AI deployment?

### Frontier Models

How should increasingly capable AI systems be evaluated before deployment into critical workflows?

## Long-Term Goal

Develop evidence-based recommendations that help organisations adopt AI safely, responsibly, and effectively.
